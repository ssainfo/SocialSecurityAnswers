Investment Options

401(k)

Employer-sponsored, pre-tax plan. 2025 limit: $23,500.

Example 1: Jane saves $500/month for 20 years at 7% = ~$260,000.

Example 2: Tom maxes out at $23,500/year for 10 years at 8% = ~$349,000.

Roth IRA

After-tax, tax-free growth. 2025 limit: $7,000 ($8,000 if 50+).

Example 1: Mike invests $5,000/year for 30 years at 7% = ~$472,000.

Example 2: Lisa, 55, puts in $8,000/year for 10 years at 6% = ~$111,000.

Stocks

Individual company shares. S&P 500 averages ~10%.

Example 1: $10,000 in 2025 grows to $25,937 in 10 years at 10%.

Example 2: Sarah buys $5,000 of SPY, gains 8%/year = $10,794 in 10 years.

ETFs & Mutual Funds

Diversified options. ETFs trade daily; mutual funds pool investments.

Example 1: $10,000 in Vanguard VOO (7%) = $19,672 in 10 years.

Example 2: John invests $3,000 in a bond fund at 5% = $4,891 in 10 years.

Real Estate

Property or REITs. Average return: 8-12%.

Example 1: $50,000 rental at 5%/year = $81,445 in 10 years.

Example 2: REIT investment of $20,000 at 10% = $51,875 in 10 years.
