Step 3: Hearing with ALJ

If your reconsideration is denied, you can request a hearing before an Administrative Law Judge (ALJ). This typically takes 12-18 months due to backlog, so file within 60 days of the reconsideration denial.

What to Expect:

  • The ALJ reviews your case independently.
  • You can present evidence and witnesses in person, by video, or by phone.
  • Consider hiring a lawyer or advocate to strengthen your case.
