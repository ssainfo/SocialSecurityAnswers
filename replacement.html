How to Replace Your Social Security Card

Lost your card? Here’s how to get a new one quickly:

Step 1: Check Eligibility

Online replacement needs a My SSA account, U.S. address, and 18+ age.

Step 2: Apply Online or By Mail

Use My SSA online or Form SS-5 via mail/office. Need ID and birth certificate.

Step 3: Receive Your Card

Mailed in 10-14 days. Call 1-800-772-1213 if delayed.

Tip: Name changes need marriage docs or court orders too!
