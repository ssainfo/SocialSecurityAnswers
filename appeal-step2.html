Step 2: File Reconsideration

If you disagree with the SSA’s initial denial, you can request a reconsideration. This is a complete review of your claim by someone who wasn’t involved in the first decision. You must file within 60 days of receiving the denial notice.

How to File:

  • Online: Use the SSA’s online appeal system at SSA Online Appeals.
  • By Mail or In-Person: Complete and submit Form SSA-561 (Request for Reconsideration).
  • Include any new evidence (e.g., updated medical records) with your request.

Resources:
