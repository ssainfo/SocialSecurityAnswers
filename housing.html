Housing Assistance Guide 2025

Explore HUD’s options to help you afford housing this year.

HUD Housing Assistance Overview

HUD helps low-income families, elderly, and disabled individuals afford housing in 2025. Here’s how:

Types of Assistance

Section 8 Vouchers

Pays part of your rent based on income (30% typically). Apply through your local PHA. Waitlists may apply.

Key Fact: Section 8 serves over 2 million households annually.

Public Housing

Affordable apartments or homes managed by PHAs. Income-based rent, often for seniors or disabled.

Home Repairs & Loans

Title I loans or local grants help fix homes. Contact your PHA or HUD office.

Eligibility

To qualify, you must meet these criteria:

  • Income: Below 50-80% of area median (varies).
  • Status: U.S. citizens or eligible immigrants.

Example: Lisa’s Eligibility

Lisa, 70, earns $15,000/year in a city where 50% AMI is $20,000. She qualifies for Section 8.

How to Apply

Find your local PHA via HUD’s PHA Locator. Apply directly.

  1. Contact your PHA by phone or online.
  2. Submit income proof and ID.
  3. Join a waitlist if needed—approval varies by area.
