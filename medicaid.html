Medicaid Basics in 2025

Medicaid is a joint federal-state program providing health coverage to low-income individuals‚Äîchildren, pregnant women, elderly, disabled, and more. In 2025, it includes programs like SSNAP, QMB, and extensive benefits, often overlapping with SSI, Medicare, and SNAP.
