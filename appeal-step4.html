Step 4: Appeals Council

If the ALJ denies your claim, you can request a review by the SSA Appeals Council. This is the final step within the SSA process, and you must file within 60 days of the hearing decision.

What to Know:

  • The Council reviews for legal errors or policy issues, not new evidence (unless exceptional).
  • If denied here, you can file a lawsuit in federal court.
  • Processing can take several months.
