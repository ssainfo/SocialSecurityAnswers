🧓 Social Security Is Only One Piece – Why Younger Workers Need Personal Savings Too

Social Security replaces about 40% of pre-retirement income. The rest must come from your own savings. Perfect for millennials and Gen Z visiting today.

Rule of Thumb

Aim to save 15–20% of your income starting in your 20s. Social Security alone won’t be enough.

401(k) + IRA Combo
