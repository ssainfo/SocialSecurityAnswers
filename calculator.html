How These Estimates Work

  • SSI: Federal Benefit Rate ($943 individual / $1,415 couple) minus countable income. Deeming applies for children under 18.
  • SSDI: Rough estimate based on years worked and average earnings (actual requires SSA record).
  • Retirement: Very approximate PIA calculation (90/32/15 bend points) — real value from my Social Security.
  • Medicare: Eligibility at 65 or after 24 months SSDI; standard Part B $185/month + possible IRMAA.

For accurate numbers, create a my Social Security account or contact SSA at 1-800-772-1213.
