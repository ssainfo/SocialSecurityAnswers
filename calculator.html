How It Works

Using 2025 SSA rules: SSI max is $967 (single) or $1,450 (couple), reduced by countable income (earned: $65 + half remainder; unearned: $20 off). For kids under 18, parental income is deemed after allocations ($484/child). SSDI and Retirement are estimated from work history. Learn more.
