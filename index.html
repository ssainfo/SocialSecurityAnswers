Social Security Card Replacement OR First-Time SSN Application in 2026

Lost, stolen, or damaged card? Brand new SSN for immigrants? Both are free. You usually don't need the physical card if you know your SSN - but here's the complete, up-to-date process.

✓ First-Time SSN Application (New Immigrants & Visa Holders)

Most new arrivals must apply in person after 10 days in the U.S. Free • 7-14 business days by mail.

Wait 10 Days After Entry

Allow DHS/SEVIS records to update.

Gather Original Documents

Passport + I-94 + I-797 (or I-20/DS-2019/EAD) + job/school letter.

Start Online & Schedule Appointment

ssa.gov - "Apply for a Social Security Number" → system directs you to ICON appointment.

Attend Appointment

Bring ALL originals. SSA verifies and issues SSN immediately if needed for work/school.

Receive Card

Mailed to your U.S. address in 7-14 business days.

Pro Tips: Always use originals. Check I-94 first at i94.cbp.dhs.gov. Bring job offer or school letter for faster processing.

Replacement Process for Existing SSN Holders

Limits: 3 per year • 10 lifetime (exceptions for name/work changes). Processing: ~10 business days.

Check Eligibility

U.S. citizens 18+ with no changes → online

Apply

my Social Security or Form SS-5 + documents.

Receive

Card mailed. Call 1-800-772-1213 if delayed past 3 weeks.

H-1B Specialty Occupation Visa SSN Guide

After I-797 Approval

Wait for I-94 to update (usually 1-3 days after entry).

Required Documents

Passport + H-1B visa stamp + I-94 + I-797 Approval Notice + LCA.

Schedule ICON Appointment

Start at ssa.gov → system routes to in-person appointment.

At SSA

SSA issues SSN immediately for work authorization.

POMS Reference: RM 00203.100 • Pro Tip: Bring original LCA. Processing usually 7-10 days by mail.

F-1 Student / OPT / CPT SSN Guide

Wait 10 Days After Entry

SEVIS must update.

Required Documents

Passport + I-20 with OPT/CPT endorsement + I-94 + EAD (if OPT) + school letter.

Appointment

ICON appointment required.

POMS Reference: RM 00203.100 • Pro Tip: School letter must confirm enrollment and that SSN is needed for work/scholarship.

J-1 Exchange Visitor SSN Guide

After DS-2019 Validation

Wait 10 days after entry.

Required Documents

Passport + DS-2019 + I-94 + sponsor/employer letter.

Appointment

ICON appointment required.

POMS Reference: RM 00203.100 • Pro Tip: J-2 dependents also eligible with same process.
