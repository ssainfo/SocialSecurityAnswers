Replace Your Social Security Card or Documents

Important: You can replace your lost or damaged Social Security card for free — but there are limits and requirements.

Who Can Request a Replacement?

  • U.S. citizens and non-citizens authorized to work
  • Limits: Usually 3 per year, 10 lifetime (exceptions apply)

Documents Needed

  • Proof of identity (driver's license, passport, etc.)
  • Proof of citizenship or immigration status if not already in SSA records

How to Apply

  1. Online (ssa.gov) if you have a my Social Security account
  2. By mail or in person at your local SSA office
  3. Form SS-5 (Application for a Social Security Card)

Warning: Never pay a third party to replace your card—it's free through SSA. Beware of scams.
